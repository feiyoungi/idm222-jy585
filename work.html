Le Cat Cafe Redesign

User Interface Designing, Prototyping

DATE: 2022-2023

ROLE: UX/UI designer

TOOLS: Figma

OVERVIEW: In this project, I analyzed design principles and reconfigured the Le Cat Café’s website, focusing on the homepage and an inner page ("Ways to Help the Cats" under the "Help Us" section). My goal was to streamline and simplify the user experience by minimizing complexity and wordiness while ensuring the retention of crucial information. Additionally, the goal is also to capture users’ interest in adopting or donating to the cause through the incorporation of softer design choices (multiple rounded edges, warm/soothing colors).

Notan Design

Photoshop, Multimedia

DATE: 2023-2024

ROLE: Designer, Photoshop Editor

TOOLS: Paper, scissors, glue, Photoshop

OVERVIEW: In this project, I worked with ambiguous and symbolic shapes taken from the movie "Good Night, and Good Luck", a 2005 film by George Clooney. It is a multimium project that moves from a physical project which involves drawing, cutting, glueing, and creating a composition of shapes with inversion, and moves to a digital product editted in Photoshop. In Photoshop, I worked with tools such as the blending function and shape maker in order to elevate my physical design.
