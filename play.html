The Midnight Snacker: A Kelpie Kitchen Mixer

Product designing, Ideating, Storyboarding

DATE: 2022-2023

ROLE: UX/UI designer, Product designer, Researcher

TOOLS: Drawing pad, Kitchen Mixer, People (for observation purposes)

OVERVIEW: Kitchen mixers have been around since 1918. Its function and design have greatly changed over time, but today, it still serves the common purpose of thoroughly mixing batters, doughs, and other ingredients to satisfy your taste palate. The Kelpie is modernly and most commonly known as a horse-like creature that lures people in with its beauty to later drown them. With both of these creatures and objects, the goal was to combine them and create a new, innovative kitchen mixer that retains the mutability and beauty of a Kelpie horse.

The Snack Guardian: An Arduino Project

Coding, 3D printing, Building, Designing

DATE: 2022-2023

ROLE: Designer, Coder

TOOLS: Arduino set, 3D modeling program, 3D Printer, Paint

OVERVIEW: Arduinos are open source electronic programs that can do a multitude of things with code. In this project, my goal was to create an alarm that offsets when there is motion detected. This kind of device, dressed in a Snorlax (Pokemon) skin, will act as your dutiful snack protector. Should you need to leave your snacks alone, you can turn on the alarm to alert you at thievery attempts.
